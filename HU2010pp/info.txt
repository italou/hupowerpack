HUNTING UNLIMITED 2010 POWERPACK
by ita - HU Corner @ http://hucorner.imbl.net

The HU 2010 PowerPack is the ultimate modding pack for Hunting Unlimited 2010 game. This pack contains over 36 more weapons, 7 hunting scopes to choose from, 5 new hunters, game fixes and lots more of equipment.
It also includes the HU Corner (target) Range as well as increased shooting, RF and viewing distance limits. Check Readme.txt file at the end of installation for complete details.

REQUIREMENTS:
  - Hunting Unlimited 2010 installed.
